      FAQ

      How can I start investing?

      Currently, our stock investing feature is only available for certain countries (under CySEC and FCA). You can access stock investing through our app which is available on iOS and Android devices.

      Is there a limit on how much I can invest?

      If your account is verified then there is no limit on your deposit. However, if your account is unverified then the deposit limit is 2000 euro (or the equivalent in $).

      However, if your account is unverified then you have a 15 day grace period to deposit up to 2000 euros (or the equivalent in $ and £). This is not applicable to clients in the UK, who must be verified before they can invest.

      What are the fees?

      There are no fees except for the tight spreads.

      Will I own the share?

      Yes, you will own the share and can sell it whenever you choose to.

      Does CapitalAssets.com pay dividends?

      Yes. CapitalAssets.com pays dividends. If a company in which you have shares in pays out dividends, then CapitalAssets.com will automatically credit your account with the relevant amount.

      Can I transfer the funds from my CFD to the stockbroking account?

      Yes! You can transfer funds between your CFD and stockbroking account.

      Will leverage be available on Stock investing accounts?

      Leverage is not available for stockbroking accounts, but we do hope to provide it in the long-term.

      Can I transfer my stocks from/to another broker?

      Currently you cannot transfer your stocks to another broker.

      Which stocks can I find on CapitalAssets.com?

      CapitalAssets.com provides access to popular shares on over 1000 companies from around the world. Please use the search function to find the stock you are interested in.

      Which stocks have zero commission?

      All stocks on the CapitalAssets.com platform are subject to zero commission with tight spreads.

      How long can I hold my stock positions without fees?

      As long as you like.

      Can I use the stockbroking feature in a demo account?

      Currently the stockbroking feature is not available on demo accounts.

      Which exchanges are available on CapitalAssets.com?

      CapitalAssets.com offers stocks from several European exchanges. However, please be aware that not every stock from each exchange is available on the platform.
